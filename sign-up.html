Frequently Asked Questions

What age can my child join?
Boys and girls ages 11-17 are eligible to join our troop.
Do I need camping experience?
Not at all! We teach all outdoor skills from scratch and pair new scouts with experienced buddies.
How much time commitment is required?
Weekly meetings (1 hour) plus optional monthly camping trips and activities. You participate as much as your schedule allows.
Can parents get involved?
Absolutely! We need parent volunteers for camping trips, merit badge counseling, committee positions, and more.
